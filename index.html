Dellwood Disc Golf Course

Location: Lockport, Illinois

Dellwood is a destination course. The scenery is gorgeous, the course design is very fun, and provides a great challenge. There are 18 regular holes with 9 alternates allowing for a 27 hole game that flows pretty well. There are plenty of wooded technical holes, plus flat wide open field bomb shots as well. The field is very wide open and seems to have swirling wind even when it's calm.

Rank in World's Best Disc Golf Courses 2021: #8

Rank in World's Best Disc Golf Courses 2020: #5

Holes: 27. Notably, 11 of those holes have secondary pin locations that are permanently in play, making for a wide array of layouts players can choose from.

Year Opened: 2014

Designer: Sean Callahan

Cost: Free
